
* **Stakeholder Identification** Who has a vested interest in the project? Understanding their needs and expectations is crucial.
  * Diverse stakeholder landscape uncovering: AI can analyze vast project data (emails, chats) to identify stakeholders beyond usual sources. This ensures a broader range of voices are considered, reducing bias in decision-making.
  * Bias awareness: AI can analyze data across your entire company, including employee profiles and past project involvement. This helps identify stakeholders from diverse backgrounds and departments, preventing bias in stakeholder selection.
  * Indirect stakeholder identification: AI can analyze project data to identify potential stakeholders outside the immediate circle who might be indirectly impacted project's outcome.   
* **Requirements Elicitation** What functionalities should the software have? This involves gathering requirements from stakeholders.
* **Market and Competitive Analysis** What similar products exist? Understanding the market landscape helps position your project.
* **Technical Feasibility Assessment** Can this project be built with current technology and resources?
* **Risk Identification and Analysis** What are potential roadblocks, and how can we mitigate them?
* **Project Scope Definition** What features will be included, and what will be left out?
* **Business Case Development** Justify the project's value by outlining its benefits and return on investment (ROI).
* **Definition of Personas and User Journeys** Who are the target users, and what are their needs and experiences?
